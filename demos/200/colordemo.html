Color Blindness Check

Short instructions

At the top right you see a small panel. There you can enter the URL of any webpage. When you click the load button, the page will be loaded and the filter applied.
Please be patient... the filtering could take a few seconds.
You need Internet Explorer 5.0 or up and DirectX for this to work. DirectX should be standard on any modern Windows system.

After this you can use the 0%, 70% and 100% radiobuttons to switch between resp. no color-blindness, some color-blindness and total color-blindness. Please try them for this page.

Below there's an Ishihara plate commonly used to check for red/green color blindness.
At 0% a number 3 is visible for people with normal color vision. But at 100% the number 3 is not visible anymore.

Kazoo

Some of us grew up messing around with sequencers on our computers and making our own music. So it was a nice surprise when we were given the opportunity to explore how that would work for today's kids.

We sat down to work on it for a few days, and out came a web based experimental music sequencer. It's even educational!

Today that experiment lives on as Kazoo, an educational tool and part of our extensive continuing work with Blink.

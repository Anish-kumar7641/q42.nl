Tesloop

At a recent Tesla shareholders meeting, a 16 year old stood up and asked Elon Musk a question: would he be able to use the upcoming driver assistance Autopilot feature in his limo service so that he could put customers in the driver's seat?

The teenager turned out to be one of the co-founders of Tesloop, a startup building a sustainable transport platform that shuttles people between Tesla Superchargers in a Model S. We hooked up with them in Los Angeles and built the first version of their app that went from nothing to in production with Meteor in just four and a half days. The goal? Start booking trips!

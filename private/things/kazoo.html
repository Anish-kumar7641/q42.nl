Music sequencer for kids

Some of us grew up messing around with sequencers and making our own music. So it was a nice surprise to get the chance to work on a music sequencer for kids during a jumpstart for Blink. In just three days, we managed to put together a promising interactive prototype and put a demo online.

Today, the product is complete and available as Kazoo Muziekstudio!

It's not enough to have an app that helps you keep track of your life goals. It also needs to actually make you happier and more productive.

That's the goal we set out with on Monday. By Friday, we had something: Little Things, the app that helps you remember to spend time with your kids, go to the beach, read a book, and do all the other stuff you never seem to get around to.
